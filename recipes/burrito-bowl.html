Burrito Bowl

Description:

A very simple burrito bowl, based off of Qdoba bowls. You can add whatever you want to make it to your liking.

Ingredients:

  • 1 15 oz. pinto beans
  • Seasonings such as cumin, garlic powder, etc. for pinto beans
  • 1/4 cup TVP rehydrated in vegan chicken or beef broth
  • Taco seasoning
  • 1 cup cooked rice, any type works
  • 5 corn tortillas
  • Olive oil spray
  • Sea salt
  • Toppings such as vegan cheese, salsa, avocado, etc.

Directions:

  1. Empty can of pinto beans with liquid into small pot. Season to your liking; I usually do cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, and salt.
  2. Heat the beans until bubbling, then keep warm.
  3. Add reydrated TVP to a heated pan sprayed with olive oil. Season with taco seasoning.
  4. Heat the TVP until it is slightly dried. Be careful not to burn it or dry it out too much.
  5. Cut the tortillas into wedges, then place into air fryer basket. I usually do not care if they overlap, but you can do two batches if you want them more evenly crisped.
  6. Spray with olive oil and sprinkle salt evenly on top.
  7. Air fry at 375 degrees for around 8-10 minutes. They burn fast, so be sure to keep an eye on them.
  8. To assemble the bowls, split the beans and TVP into two bowls. Add 1/2 cup rice to each. Top with desired toppings, and serve with the chips.
